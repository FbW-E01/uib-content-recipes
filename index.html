PASTEL DE CHOCLO A.K.A KORN CAKE

Ingredients

  • 2 Garlic well choped
  • 2 Onions choped in lines
  • 500 gr of meat
  • 4 boild eggs
  • 800 gr of korn
  • 100 mll milk
  • 100 mll cream
  • Basil
  • TUTORIAL

Preparation

  1. chop garlics and onions
  2. heat the pan in medium fire with oil and put the gsarlic and onion
  3. once booth are mixed add the meat
  4. boil the eggs
  5. put the korn with milk and cream inside the blender and mixed for 1 minute
  6. Once the meat, garlic and onion are ready put it in a fount and cover the entire base of the fount
  7. chop the eggs and add it over the meat
  8. and finaly add the korn paste to our fount
  9. turn on the oven and put it in 200 dgr and put the korn cake inside for 25 minutes
  10. Once in ready, take it away from the oven and add some basil, now is ready
